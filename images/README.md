## Required action before public release

For each upstream RadarHD image, record:

- the exact paper page, repository path, presentation, or project page;
- the original caption, where available;
- the copyright holder;
- the license or permission supporting reuse;
- whether the image was copied unchanged or adapted.

Figures derived from SLAM-RF data require separate confirmation from the SENS
Lab even when they were generated entirely by the student group.
